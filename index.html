Bankroll guide
What is a unit?
A unit is a fixed percentage of your bankroll — usually 1-3%. If your bankroll is $1,000 and you use 2% units, 1 unit = $20.
What is Kelly Criterion?
A formula that tells you the optimal percentage of your bankroll to bet based on the odds and your estimated win probability.
Conservative vs Aggressive?
Conservative = 0.5-1 unit max. Moderate = 1-2 units. Aggressive = 2-3 units. Most sharp bettors stay at 1-2 units even on their strongest plays.
Stop loss rule
Never risk more than 10 units in a single day. If you hit your stop loss, stop betting until tomorrow.
Positive expected value (+EV)
A bet has +EV when the true probability of winning is higher than what the odds imply. Finding +EV bets consistently is the only way to beat sportsbooks long term.
